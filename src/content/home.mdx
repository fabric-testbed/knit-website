# KNIT13 | September 28, 2026

Join us for KNIT13, the next FABRIC Community Workshop, September 28, in Washington DC!

**KNIT13: Networking for the Future** brings together the FABRIC community for a day of advanced training, plenary sessions, and hands-on exploration of cutting-edge research networking. Taking place in Washington, DC, KNIT13 is co-located with the PAWR PI Meeting, creating an opportunity for cross-community engagement and collaboration. Combined meals will facilitate networking across projects.

---

**What to Expect:**

- Advanced Training & Hands-On Learning: Dive deep into FABRIC's most powerful features and emerging capabilities with expert-led technical sessions.
- Thought-Provoking Plenary Sessions: Hear from visionaries shaping the future of programmable infrastructure and next-generation networking.
- Impactful Research Showcases: Explore real-world experiments spanning scientific domains, from high-energy physics to AI-driven network optimization, and see how FABRIC is accelerating discovery.

Note: The MERIF Workshop will follow KNIT13 and the PAWR PI Meeting and will be held at the same location in Washington, DC, September 29-30, 2026.
