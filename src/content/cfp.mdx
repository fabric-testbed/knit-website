## Golden Stitch Award

FABRIC is now accepting nominations for the 2026 Golden Stitch Awards, recognizing outstanding research, experimentation, and teaching using FABRIC resources. Community members can nominate themselves or colleagues for categories including Best Published Paper, Best FABRIC Matrix, Best Experiment, and Best Classroom Use through March 31, 2026. Winners will be announced at KNIT12 and featured in a FABRIC blog post.
